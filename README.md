# 🎮 Little Nicky Multi Chat

> Display TikTok Live and Twitch chat, gifts, and events directly on your screen while you stream — no fuss, just run and go.

---

## ⬇️ Installation

1. Download `LittleNicky-MultiChat-x.x.x.exe` from the Assets section below
2. Run the `.exe` — no installation needed, it launches straight away
3. The app will appear as an overlay on your screen

---

## 🔧 OBS Setup

1. Open **OBS Studio**
2. Add a new **Browser Source**
3. Set the URL to: `http://localhost:16666`
4. Set width/height to match your stream resolution (e.g. 1920×1080)
5. Done — chat will appear in your OBS scene

---

## 🎵 TikTok Setup

1. Click the **TikTok** button in the app
2. Log in with your TikTok account in the popup window
3. Once logged in, your live chat and gifts will connect automatically when you go live

---

## 🟣 Twitch Setup

1. Click the **Twitch** button in the app
2. Authorise with your Twitch account
3. Enter your channel name
4. Chat messages will start appearing straight away

---

## 🎁 Gift Actions (TikTok)

Map TikTok gifts to sounds or keypresses in-game:

1. Open the **Gift Actions** panel in the app
2. Select a gift from the dropdown
3. Choose a WAV sound and/or set a key to press when that gift is received
4. Click **Save Gift Actions**

> 💡 You can upload your own WAV files using the **Upload WAV for Gifts** button

---

## 🔄 Auto-Update

The app checks for updates automatically on startup. When a new version is available:
- You'll get a notification
- The update downloads in the background
- The app restarts into the new version

No need to manually download each release!

---

## ❓ Common Issues

**Chat not showing in OBS?**
Make sure the app is running before OBS, and the Browser Source URL is exactly `http://localhost:16666`

**TikTok not connecting?**
You must be live on TikTok for chat to connect. Log out and back in if it keeps failing.

**App not launching?**
Try right-clicking the `.exe` and selecting **Run as Administrator**
